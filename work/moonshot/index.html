Brand Identity — Moonshot

Space has a logistics problem. Everything beyond our atmosphere costs a fortune to reach — fuel, replacement parts, critical supplies — and that cost is what has kept space small. Moonshot was built to end that.

Their electromagnetic kinetic launch system flips the economics. 94% payload-to-launch ratio. Not a marginal improvement — a generational one. The kind that turns space from a billionaire sport into an actual civilization.

By 2028, spacecraft stay in orbit longer. By 2032, the lunar economy has a pulse. By 2036, there is a living supply chain between Earth and Mars.

We built a brand that could carry that weight. Not a logo for a startup. An identity for the infrastructure of the next era — precise, inevitable, already in motion.
