the capture → convert → approve loop

1. capture (you, 2 min/day) leave raw traces while you work. screenshots, 15-sec clips, two-line notes. one folder. don’t edit, don’t think platform.
2. convert (operator + tools, 2x/week) raw → reels, captions, tweets, newsletter. published on your set schedule. tools do ~70% (AI drafts, auto-scheduler, clip cutter).
3. approve (you, 15 min/week) taste gate. approve, kill, fix tone. never touch instagram yourself.

the rule: no capture, no machine. the 2 min/day is the only thing you can’t skip.
the test: run week 1 as capture-only. publish nothing. if you leave traces consistently, you have a machine. if not, it’s your habit — fix that before hiring.
