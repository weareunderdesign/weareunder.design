10-day plan

it's all about starting big, then getting more and more focused until you create a brand that sticks

before

we treat design as problem-solving that demands taste. not committees. not consensus. taste. our sprint: one direction, one system, clear rationale. cohesion over chaos. no decision fatigue. requires the right champion. one decision maker. ceo or nobody. singular vision, not averaged opinions.

before we start, you deliver all the content. site messaging, copy, product details - signed off and final. we don't fill gaps. we don't guess. if it's not ready, we push the start date.

day 0 strategy

we kick off with a session to turn abstract ideas into something tangible. past, present, future. why? how? what? who? where? when? we tackle values, audience, competition. it's the foundation for both your brand and your product path.

day 1 research

deep dive. we uncover your point of view, approach, tone of voice. we craft a brand story. your brand and go-to-market compass. we confirm the brand story together. if we're not excited about it, we stop. clarity now saves chaos later.

day 2 design

one clear creative direction. logo, typography, color system. beyond visuals, we extend into product touchpoints, marketing assets, distribution design. we work in deep focus - no calls, no emails, just pure creation.

day 10 present

your office, your team, big reveal. we present once. you get 48 hours to give feedback - all of it, from everyone.

day 11 refine

we take your feedback and make changes based on our professional judgment. no second meeting. no discussion. we deliver the final version.

day 12 handover

keys in your hands. you learn to run it, scale it, grow it. it's graduation day, but also ignition.

ongoing

we check in with strategic guidance as the system evolves. a brand is never done.
