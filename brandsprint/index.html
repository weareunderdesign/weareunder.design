10-day plan

it's all about starting big, then getting more and more focused until you create a brand that sticks

day 0 strategy

we kick things off with a mind-bending workshop. we dig deep—why do you do what you do? who is your audience? why should anyone care? it's like speed dating, but for your brand.

day 1-3 research

we absorb, analyze, and decode. this is where raw ideas get refined into brand clarity.

day 4-9 design

we design, test, refine, repeat. no back button. no endless revisions. just high-velocity execution.

day 10 present

one presentation. no options. no indecision. just a brand built to dominate.

day 11 handover

your brand, delivered. ready to launch, grow, and own the market.
