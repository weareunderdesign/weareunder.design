10-day plan

it's all about starting big, then getting more and more focused until you create a brand that sticks

day 0 strategy

let's go. we kick off with a session to turn abstract ideas into something tangible. past, present, future. why do you do this? how? what? we tackle values, audience, competition. it's the foundation for both your brand and your product path.

day 1-3 research

deep dive. we uncover your point of view, approach, tone of voice. we craft a brand story that doubles as your product and go-to-market compass. findings, concepts, alignment, approval. clarity now saves chaos later.

day 4-9 design

one clear creative direction. logos that pop, typography that sings, colors that dazzle. and beyond visuals, we stretch into product touchpoints, marketing assets, distribution design. we work in deep focus mode - no calls, no emails, just pure creation. our sprint process is unique - one direction, one comprehensive system, backed by rationale. this creates cohesion and removes decision fatigue.

day 10 present

drumroll. your office, your team, our reveal. not just a brand book, but a company system: story, visuals, product, market entry. it's your brand system's birthday and your company's launch pad.

day 11 handover

keys in your hands. you learn to run it, scale it, grow it. we refine last parts together. it's graduation day, but also ignition.

handshake

congrats. you now own a full system! strategy, story, design, product, market. we hand you the keys, show you how to run it, and make sure it drives smooth. this isn't goodbye, it's the start of the ride.

ongoing

we stay close, as your system evolves. updates, check-ins. a brand is never done, and neither is a company.
