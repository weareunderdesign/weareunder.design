we're under. simple on the surface, complex underneath. so we made a font that works the same way. one font. does all the work. adapts to tiny screens, figma files, pdf forms, whatever you throw at it. auto line height. auto spacing. auto kerning. variable. open source. you know inter? helvetica? they're fine. but we wanted something that actually works as hard as we do. under is that font.

alternative letter

The font offers alternative letterforms that can be accessed manually for stylistic variation

Superscript and Subscript

Superscript (sups): raises text to superscript position (e.g., x²). Subscript (subs): lowers text to subscript position (e.g., H₂O).

Precision and control

custom spacing pairs for precision, and auto letter-spacing for balanced flow. Auto-letter spacing is adjusted per weight for optimal readability.

precise and simple

clean lines, easy to read. no fancy stuff just clarity and function.
